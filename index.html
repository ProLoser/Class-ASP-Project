ASP.NET and Nested Forms

So you can't put a form inside a form for sending data to another page, but moving the new form to the end of the file makes the page look ugly? Well I got the solution for YOU:

<asp:Button runat="server" PostBackUrl="~/addcart.aspx" Text="Add to Cart" />

Specifically that PostBackUrl part will let you change what page the button sends the user to! No second form needed whatsoever!
